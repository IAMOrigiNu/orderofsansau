The Sacred Mission

We are not building a retreat center. We are establishing a tribe.

The Order of San Sau Earth Sanctuary is the living embodiment of a world where abundance in health and wealth is achieved through authenticity, self-determination, and collective stewardship of the land. We live on Delley ancestral land in Dubach, Louisiana — 60 acres of sacred timberland — where we are rooting an interfaith, women-led, Sufi-path community in the ancient arts of earth care, sacred ceremony, and mutual flourishing.


This is Afrofuturism meeting ancient wisdom. This is Heaven being grounded in Earth. Your investment is not a donation — it is a stake in something eternal.

What We Are Building

Seven pillars of a self-sustaining sacred community — each one a living expression of interdependence between people, land, and Spirit.

Sacred Land

Permanent stewardship of 60 acres of Delley ancestral timberland — forest gardens, permaculture systems, and sacred ceremony grounds.

Earth-Built Structures

Strawbale and cobb construction — ancient techniques that build from what the earth provides. Permanent, beautiful, and sovereign.

Resilience School

The Soil & Souls Resilience School — grandmother-led cultural transmissions, rooting the next generation in original goodness.

Sacred Practice

Daily meditation, Sufi path teachings, and the Ancient Meditation of the Lotus Chakra — the spiritual spine of the community.

Ecological Systems

Composting, beekeeping, forest gardens, water stewardship, and participation in the Time to Restore multi-organizational project.

Tribal Economy

A trust-structured, interdependent economy — honoring the worker, the land, and the relationship, cultivating intergenerational health and wealth.

Women of the Order on the sacred Delley ancestral land, Dubach, Louisiana
